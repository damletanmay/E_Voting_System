How to Vote ??

  1. Click here or go to Navigation bar to Login.
  2. Enter your Voter ID, then click on Get OTP button.
  3. OTP will be sent to your Registered Mobile Number, enter that into field and press Log In button.
  4. There will be a table having details about Upcoming elections, Click on the link Vote in the last column. Click there to give your vote. It will redirect you to Voting page.
    NOTE: You can give vote only in the described time.
  5. Select a Candidate of your choice and click on the button Confirm Your Vote.

How to Register For Being A Candidate ?

  1. Go to Election Office at your nearest branch.
  2. Verify Required Documents like voter id, aadhar card,etc.
  3. Once Verified, Candidate shall go to the enrollment department for Candidacy in Election.
  4. If the Candidate Wishes for Candidacy then, above steps must be done before the election begins.

Requirements For Being A Candidate

  1. Candidate Must Be A Citizen Of India.
  2. Candidate Must meet age requirements for particular elections.
  3. Candidate Must bring a image media of his/her party logo.
  4. Candidate's District & State Must Be Same as where the election is being held.
