How to Vote ??

  1. Click here or go to Navigation bar to Login.
  2. Enter your Voter ID, then click on Get OTP button.
  3. OTP will be sent to your Registered Mobile Number, enter that into field and press Log In button.
  4. There will be a table having details about Upcoming elections, Click on the link Vote in the last column. Click there to give your vote. It will redirect you to Voting page.
    NOTE: You can give vote only in the described time.
  5. Select a Party of your choice and click on the button Confirm your Vote.
  6. If you want to clear your selection, click on Clear Selection.
  7. That's It !!!
